Understand your audiences: Health-medical information-seeking-behavior studies at pubmed.gov

2026-04-04 – Use this report to understand information-needs, information-seeking, and information-use behaviors for various audiences for biomedical information. Example: A link for "caregivers" would run the following search strategy at https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov: Caregivers[Mesh] AND ("Information Seeking Behavior"[Mesh] OR "information seeking"[Title] OR "information needs"[Title] OR "user stud*"[Title/Abstract] OR "user research"[Title/Abstract]) AND "last 6 years"[PDat].
